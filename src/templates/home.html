All your FlashCard - Online!

Never forget anything!

FlashCards are mostly used to consolidate your vocabulary in any languages, but as this method is generic, you can use it to remember absolutely anything. Remembering and increasing your general culture becomes easy.

Your memory uses a two way binding. You link an idea or word in two way.

For instance if you're a tennis fan, and I'm asking you what represents the number 20, you will probably never guess. But if I'm asking to you how many times Federer won a grand slam, you will find easily that it's 20!

The method used at IziDict.com heavily rely on how the memory works.

You create your FlashCards online (recto and verso), and can take quizzes about them. If you pass the quizz about a FlashCard, it will move to higher bucket of FlashCard, that will be asked to you later. If you fail, the FlashCard will be moved to a lower bucket of FlashCard, that will be asked to you sooner.

This method has been proven to be very effective for various subject like:

  • increase your general culture
  • build your vocabulary
  • learn a new language
